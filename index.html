Four ways of the ghost. One legend.

Samurai

Let the blade decide.

Melee Armor Stance

The Samurai excels in direct, honorable combat. High defense values and devastating katana combos make this class ideal for players who prefer deliberate, powerful strikes over evasion. A warrior of iron resolve and unyielding code.

⬆ Strengths

  • Highest base armor rating
  • Access to exclusive Stance abilities
  • Devastating parry windows
  • Strong against armored enemies

⬇ Weaknesses

  • Low mobility compared to Shinobi
  • Struggles against ranged swarms
  • Resource-heavy stance management

🗡 Core Techniques

  • Iron Will — Unstoppable charge stance
  • Deflect Mastery — Perfect parry window
  • Honored Blade — Katana damage boost
  • Heaven's Descent — AOE katana slam
弓
🏹

Archer

Every arrow carries death.

Ranged Precision Stealth

Silent. Patient. Lethal. The Archer controls engagements from afar, rewarding patience and positioning. High burst damage paired with stealth integration makes this class a nightmare for any enemy unlucky enough to be targeted.

⬆ Strengths

  • Highest single-target damage
  • Exceptional stealth synergy
  • Explosive arrow variants
  • Long-range zone control

⬇ Weaknesses

  • Vulnerable in close quarters
  • Limited ammo reserves
  • Needs high ground positioning

🗡 Core Techniques

  • Eagle Eye — Extended aim duration
  • Breathless — Slows time on aim
  • Phantom Arrow — Silent shot
  • Volley — Multi-arrow scatter shot
傭兵
⚙

Mercenary

Brutality beats hesitation.

Hybrid Explosives Versatile

Ruthless adaptability. The Mercenary is built for those who refuse to be boxed in. Explosive utility, hybrid weapon access, and raw aggression make this class the most versatile option for any scenario or battlefield.

⬆ Strengths

  • Access to all weapon categories
  • Explosive and trap utility
  • High adaptability mid-fight
  • Best multi-target clearance

⬇ Weaknesses

  • No class specialization bonus
  • Gear cost is highest
  • Stealth is limited

🗡 Core Techniques

  • Iron Gambit — Risk/reward stance
  • Smoke Bomb — AoE blind
  • Grenadier — Explosive cooldown cut
  • Brutalize — Executions restore health
忍
🌑

Shinobi

Strike fast. Vanish faster.

Stealth Mobility Assassination

The Shinobi thrives on stealth and mobility. Most of the Shinobi’s power comes from silent assassinations, fast repositioning, and striking before the enemy has time to react. Fragile but deadly, the class rewards precision, patience, and mechanical mastery.

⬆ Strengths

  • Fastest base movement speed
  • Best assassination damage
  • Grapple and traversal tools
  • Stealth chain kills

⬇ Weaknesses

  • Lowest base health pool
  • Punished heavily for exposure
  • High skill floor requirement

🗡 Core Techniques

  • Phantom Step — Instant repositioning
  • Death Blossom — Rapid dagger flurry
  • Vanish — Combat stealth reset
  • Kunai Storm — Ranged projectile burst
