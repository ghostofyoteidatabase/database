Onryō Tokens Core Mechanic
👁️
What are Onryō Tokens?
Onryō Tokens represent your lifeline against the supernatural forces of Yōtei. Each token is a spiritual anchor — spend them to recover from lethal encounters. The harder the difficulty, the fewer tokens you receive, demanding greater skill, coordination, and precision from your team.
Token Allotment by Difficulty
🥉 Bronze — 3 Tokens
🥈 Silver — 2 Tokens
🥇 Gold — 1 Token
💎 Platinum — 0 Tokens
Difficulty Tokens Effect Risk Level
🥉 Bronze 3 3 revival opportunities per run Low
🥈 Silver 2 2 revival opportunities per run Medium
🥇 Gold 1 1 revival — use wisely High
💎 Platinum 0 One life only — no second chances Extreme

Lore The World of Yōtei
📜 The Legend of the Spider
Long before the first sword was forged in the shadow of Mount Yōtei, the Spider wove its web across the spirit realm and the mortal world alike. It is neither demon nor god — it is older than both. Ancient texts discovered in the ruins of the Ainu sanctuaries describe it as a weaver of fates, a collector of final moments.

When the black powder merchants disturbed the sealed valleys, they tore the barrier between worlds. The Jubokko trees drank their blood. The Onryō — vengeful spirits of fallen warriors — marched again beneath the Spider's command. Three safeguards remained, and one by one, they fell.

Now the Spider waits at the center of its web. Patient. Eternal. Hungry.
— Recovered from Ainu Settlement Records, The Spider Arc
🌸
Mount Yōtei
A dormant volcano in Hokkaido, revered by the Ainu people as a sacred site and home to powerful nature spirits. The game's supernatural events center around its ancient seal network.
🌳
The Jubokko
Blood-drinking trees of Japanese folklore. In the Yōtei Legends canon, they grow on ancient battlefields where the dead outnumber the living, absorbing the vitality of those who pass beneath their branches.
👻
Onryō Spirits
Vengeful ghosts born from unresolved deaths and intense negative emotion. In Legends mode, they serve as primary foot soldiers of the supernatural forces threatening the Yōtei region.
