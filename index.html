⚔️ Campaign Database

The Spider

Arc I — Campaign

The Spider arc is the first campaign in Ghost of Yōtei Legends. It consists of three story missions and one Incursion finale. Each mission is playable across four difficulty tiers — Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum — each offering scaling XP rewards and increasing challenge. Complete all three story missions to unlock the Incursion.

🛡️ Survival Mode

Survival Missions

Endless wave defense — escalating challenge

Survival missions challenge players to defend objectives against escalating enemy waves. Each difficulty tier requires a higher Ki level and rewards progressively more XP. Survival missions support 1–4 players cooperatively.

💀
Nightmare Mode
All Nightmare activities — Campaign, Survival, and Raid — require a minimum Ki of 110. This is the maximum Ki cap. Nightmare is the hardest modifier available and applies to all game modes simultaneously.

🥷 Class Database

Playable Classes

Choose Your Path · 4 Available

Ghost of Yōtei Legends features four distinct classes, each with their own role in co-op play. Classes differ in playstyle, techniques, strengths, and recommended co-op roles. All four classes can complete all game modes including Campaign, Survival, Raid, and Incursion.

⚔️
Samurai
Frontline · Damage · Tank

A powerful warrior built for direct combat. The Samurai excels at close-range katana combat, crowd control, and absorbing heavy hits for the team. Strong in all modes.

Melee Crowd Control High Defense Katana
🏹
Archer
Ranged · Support · DPS

A precision ranged fighter who controls space and supports teammates from a distance. The Archer uses bow techniques to deal consistent damage and apply status effects.

Ranged Precision Status Effects Bow
🗡️
Mercenary
Brawler · Off-Tank · Aggressive

A high-aggression fighter who prioritizes raw damage output and battlefield pressure. The Mercenary sacrifices some defense for offensive power and is effective in all game modes.

Aggressive High Damage Brawler Off-Tank
🥷
Shinobi
Stealth · Assassin · Utility

A fast, evasive operative specializing in assassination, flanking, and utility abilities. The Shinobi can eliminate priority targets quickly and reposition rapidly in high-pressure scenarios.

Stealth Fast Kunai Flanker

🏆 Trophy Database

Ghost of Yōtei Trophies

6 Trophies Available

Ghost of Yōtei Legends features six trophies tied to key progression milestones. Each trophy represents a significant gameplay achievement. Below is a complete breakdown of all currently known trophies.

🏆
Tale of Revenge
🥇 Gold Trophy
Awarded for completing a major step in the revenge storyline of the game. Tied to campaign progression.
🥈
Student No More
🥈 Silver Trophy
Awarded for surpassing the training phase and demonstrating combat mastery. Tied to class progression and skill unlocks.
🏆
Vengeance Fulfilled
🥇 Gold Trophy
Awarded for completing the vengeance arc of the game's main narrative. A key story milestone trophy.
💀
Ghost Unyielding
💀 Nightmare Trophy
Awarded for completing a major challenge without yielding — most likely tied to Nightmare difficulty or a no-death run milestone.
🥈
No More Lessons
🥈 Silver Trophy
Awarded for completing all training requirements or surpassing a mentor-level challenge within the game's progression system.
✨
Kaibyaku
⭐ Special Trophy
A special trophy — "Kaibyaku" (開闢) means the genesis or creation of the world. Likely tied to a significant story moment or major milestone unlocking new content.
✨ Progression System

XP & Ki System

Difficulty Tiers · Ki Requirements · Scaling

Ghost of Yōtei Legends uses two linked progression systems: XP (Experience Points) earned from completing missions at various difficulty tiers, and Ki — the primary power level stat that determines which content you can viably access. Higher Ki allows you to take on higher difficulty tiers and more challenging game modes.
